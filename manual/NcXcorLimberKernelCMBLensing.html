Description

The kernel is given by \begin{equation} W^{\kappa_\mathrm{CMB}} (z) = \frac{3}{2} \frac{\Omega_m H_0^2}{c} \frac{(1+z)}{H(z)} \chi(z) \frac{\chi(z_*) - \chi(z)}{\chi(z_*)}. \end{equation}
