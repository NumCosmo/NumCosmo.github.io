nc_halo_mass_function_dv_dzdomega ()

gdouble
nc_halo_mass_function_dv_dzdomega (NcHaloMassFunction *mfp,
                                   NcHICosmo *cosmo,
                                   gdouble z);

This function computes the comoving volume (flat universe) element per unit solid angle $d\Omega$ given z , namely, $$\frac{\mathrm{d}^2V}{\mathrm{d}z\mathrm{d}\Omega} = \frac{c}{H(z)} D_c^2(z),$$ where $H(z)$ is the Hubble function and $D_c$ is the comoving distance.
