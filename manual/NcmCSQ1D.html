Description

The system: \begin{align} q^\prime &= \frac{\Pi_q}{m}, \Pi_q^\prime &= m\nu^2q. \end{align}

\begin{equation} \xi = \ln (m\nu) \end{equation}

\begin{equation} F^n = \left(\frac{1}{2\nu}\frac{\partial}{\partial t}\right)^n \xi. \end{equation}
