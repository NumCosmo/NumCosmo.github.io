Description

The kernel is given by \begin{equation} W^{\kappa_{\mathrm{gal}}} = \frac{3}{2} \frac{\Omega_m H_0^2}{c} \frac{(1+z)}{H(z)} \chi(z) \int dz^\prime \frac{dn}{dz^\prime} \frac{\chi(^\prime) - \chi(z)}{\chi(^\prime)} \end{equation}

where $\frac{dn}{dz}$ is the redshift distribution of galaxies.
