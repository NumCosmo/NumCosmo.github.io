Description

The kernel is given by \begin{equation} W^{T_\mathrm{ISW}} (z) = \frac{3 \Omega_m H_0^2}{c^2 (l + 1/2)^2} \chi^2(z) \frac{\rm{d}}{\rm{d}z}\left((1 + z)D(z)\right). \end{equation}
